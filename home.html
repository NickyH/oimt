The Initiative Management Tool provides a central portal for capturing, assessing and implementing new business initiatives within Downer. To submit an idea simply register with your Downer email address, then login and tell us your idea. Once an initiative is captured in the system it will go into the ideas register where it will undergo a process of assessment by the initiatives team. You can track your ideas and view all current and past ideas that have been submitted.

Track My Ideas

If you've already submitted an idea you can track the progress of your idea. If your idea hasn't already been assessed you can edit your idea. To submit a new idea you need to first login and then you can tell us your idea.

Browse All Ideas

To browse new ideas that have been submitted you can view the ideas list. You can see which ideas have been submitted or approved. If you want to search past ideas you can view all closed or archived. if you get inspired and want to submit your own idea, you need to login first, then you can tell us your idea.

Manage My Projects

Are you a project manager and have been assigned a project in IMT? First you just need to login, and then you can manage your projects.

Submit an R&D Project

Use the Research & Development Portal to submit, manage and view R&D projects. You can also manage R&D projects within a Downer project during the execution phase. If you are submitting an idea and already know there may be an R&D component to the project, you should indicate this in your idea submission.
